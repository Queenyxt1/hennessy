A Few Words About Us

Hennessy Cognac was founded by Richard Hennessy in 1765. It is one of the three major cognac brands in the world. Hennessy Cognac has been founded for more than 250 years, and its blending skills have been passed down to this day through Hennessy and Fairworth families.
